About Tung Sahur Clicker

Tung Sahur Clicker is a culturally themed incremental game where timing and rhythm drive your progress. Click along with the in-game meter to earn Sahur Power and bonus Rhythm Points. Sahur Power lets you buy and upgrade automated Wakers like drummers and speakers, while Rhythm Points unlock temporary buffs that boost output fourfold.

Key Features:

  • Rhythm Meter: Rewards precise timing for extra points.

  • Waker Automation: Generates Sahur Power even when idle.

  • Blessings: Temporary multipliers for massive gains.

  • Daybreak Prestige: Reset for Barakah Tokens to unlock permanent upgrades.

For fans of quirky clickers, also try Chicken Jockey Clicker for more addictive tapping fun.
